Question - About Dataset

1. What is the dataset?

2. Why is the dataset collected?




Answer

1. Dataset is a part of AUSTRALIAN BUREAU OF STATISTICS - Census of Population and Housing. Dataset G23 is about 'RELATIONSHIP IN HOUSEHOLD BY AGE BY SEX'.

2. Dataset is collected for government/non-government organisations to provide appropriate service efficiently to participated area/ group of participants or citizens. Especially for G23, Dataset is collected to count the number of people in a private household. The collected data is then utilised for various purposes.
